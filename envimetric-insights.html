See how EnviMetric focuses your effort

Use EnviMetric to plan site investigation, perform a fast computerized study of an area to compare to sampling, and create what-if scenarios. EnviMetric is a data driven approach that compiles all of the discoveries and learning from thousands of prior sites into a single model that describes where the effected area is - how far from the origin, how widely is it dispersed, and how deep does it go. By using this machine learning approach, you can leverage all of the millions of bits of information gained from years of site investigation, and fit it to your target site.
